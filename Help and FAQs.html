How we can help ?




  • How do I download the AFC Richmond APP?

    It’s really easy to download the AFC Richmond App from your smartphone or tablet device. If you’re using an iPhone, iPad or Apple device just head to the App store. If you’re on an Android device, simply go to the Play Store.

  • How can I download a picture from gallery?

    To download a picture from gallery in our website, users can just right-click on it and select “Save image” from the menu.

  • Is there a restaurant or cafe at stadium?

    Yes, the Blue Café, is open to the public seven days a week – except matchdays – and offers varied breakfast and lunch menus.

  • How can I join Fans Association club?

    By contact us in our email "fansassociation@afcrichmond.my".

  • Can I watch live games in the website?

    No, you only able to watch the latest results.
